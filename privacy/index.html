Privacy & Cookies

What we track,
and what we don't.

A short, plain-language rundown of the cookies this site uses.

Cookies we use

This site uses one category of cookie: analytics, via Google Analytics. These cookies help us understand which pages get visited and how people navigate the site, so we can improve it. They don't identify you personally, and we don't use them for advertising.

Your choice

When you first visit, a small banner asks whether you're OK with analytics cookies. If you decline, no analytics cookies are set and nothing is sent to Google Analytics. You can change your mind at any time using the "Cookie settings" link in the footer of any page — it re-opens the banner so you can update your choice.

What Google Analytics collects

If you accept, Google Analytics collects standard usage data: pages viewed, time on site, general location (country/city level), device and browser type, and how you arrived at the site. For details on how Google itself handles this data, see Google's Privacy Policy.

No other tracking

Outside of the analytics cookie described above, this site sets no other cookies — no advertising trackers, no third-party marketing pixels, no cross-site tracking.
