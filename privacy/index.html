Privacy policy

How Naja Projects collects and uses data across this site.

Naja Projects ("we", "us") runs this site and the ventures listed on it: Naja-ware, Naja Coaching, Naja Build and our pipeline concepts. This page explains what we collect when you visit, and why.

What we collect

Basic analytics (pages viewed, referring site, device and browser type) collected only if you accept analytics cookies. If you get in touch through the contact page or email, we keep the name, email address and message you send us so we can reply.

Cookies

We use a single analytics cookie to understand how visitors move through the site. Nothing is set until you accept it in the banner shown on your first visit. Declining means no analytics cookie is stored, and the rest of the site works exactly the same either way. You can change your choice any time via "Cookie settings" in the footer.

How we use it

Analytics data helps us see which pages are useful and which aren't. Contact details are used only to respond to you and, if relevant, route your message to the right person across our ventures. We don't sell or share your information with third parties for marketing.

Your rights

You can ask us what we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it, at any time. Just email one of the addresses on the contact page.

Questions

If anything here is unclear, reach out. See the contact page for the right person to ask.
