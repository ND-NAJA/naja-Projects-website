Naja-ware.
Software personalized for you.

Naja-ware designs and builds the websites, apps, and SaaS platforms that a growing business needs.

  • Portal is a business operating system we built for our own project development work, then opened up for other teams to run on too.

  • Members ask each other questions, find people within the app, and organize events together across companies.

  • Companies present their work, and prospective clients browse it to find the right fit.

  • Landing pages and full marketing sites built to convert, and easy for your own team to update after launch.

  • Purpose-built apps for a specific workflow, not a generic template stretched to fit.

  • Multi-tenant products with ongoing support after launch, not a one time handoff.

  • We stay on after launch: bug fixes, new features, and whatever comes up as your business grows.

Three ways to run on Naja-ware

Portal handles project tracking, client coordination, and day-to-day operations for teams and companies.

Momentum connects entrepreneurs with one another. Members ask each other questions, find people within the app, and organize events together.

Vision is where entrepreneurs showcase their projects. Companies present their work, and prospective clients browse it to find the right fit.
