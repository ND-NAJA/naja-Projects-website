Solid/ghost/outline call-to-action button used across the Naja Projects site for hero CTAs, section CTAs, and nav.

```jsx
<Button variant="primary" href="/contact/">Book a coaching call</Button>
<Button variant="ghost" href="#ventures">See what we're building ↓</Button>
<Button variant="outline" href="/contact/">Start a project</Button>
```

Variants: `primary` (solid orange, lifts 2px on hover), `ghost` (outlined, brightens border+text on hover, used next to a primary button), `outline` (small, used only in the nav bar CTA).
