One login. Every part of the business, in sync — documents, planning, bookkeeping, materials and people.

Naja Portal was built for teams who were tired of running their business across five different tools. Documents, planning, bookkeeping, materials, and people — it's all one system, and it grows with you from a two-person crew to a full company.

  • → Planning that connects sales, delivery and site work
  • → Bookkeeping and invoicing, built in, not bolted on
  • → Materials and stock tracking per project
  • → Personnel scheduling and time oversight
