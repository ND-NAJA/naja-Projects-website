Portal.

Project tracking, client coordination, and day to day operations for teams and companies.

Portal is the operating system behind Naja Projects. It handles project tracking, client coordination, and day-to-day operations for teams and companies, built for the way a hands-on business actually runs.

  • →Project tracking across every job and client, in one shared view
  • →Client coordination, so nothing falls through the cracks between teams
  • →Day to day operations: scheduling, documents and reporting in one place

Built for teams and companies

Portal started as a tool for our own project development work, because nothing on the market fit how a small, hands-on business actually operates. Now it runs Naja Build and Naja Coaching too.
